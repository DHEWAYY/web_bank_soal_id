A. Pilihan Ganda

1.

Read the text carefully! **GRAND OPENING!** **FLAVOR PARADISE RESTAURANT** Come and taste our delicious local and international cuisines. We provide the best romantic atmosphere for you and your partner. **Special Promo:** Buy 1 Get 1 Free for Steak Menu on Saturday Night! Located at: Jl. Merdeka No. 45, Bandung. Open daily: 10.00 am - 10.00 pm What is the writer's intention in writing the text?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: B

Tujuan utama teks iklan (advertisement) adalah untuk mempromosikan atau membujuk (to persuade/to promote) orang agar membeli produk atau jasa yang ditawarkan.

2.

Refer to the text in question no. 1. Who would likely be most interested in the 'Special Promo'?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: B

Teks menyebutkan 'romantic atmosphere' dan promo 'Buy 1 Get 1 Free... on Saturday Night', yang sangat identik dengan pasangan (couple) yang ingin makan malam.

3.

Refer to the text in question no. 1. 'We provide the best romantic **atmosphere**...' The underlined word is closest in meaning to...

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: C

Atmosphere dalam konteks tempat/restoran bersinonim dengan suasana atau 'Ambience'.

4.

Read the text! **SALE! SALE! SALE!** **SMARTPHONE X-200** - 6.5 inch display - 128 GB Storage - Triple Camera - Long-lasting battery (5000 mAh) **Rp 2.500.000 (Normal Price Rp 3.500.000)** Offer ends on January 31st. Visit 'Tech World' Store now! How much can we save if we buy the phone during the sale?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: B

Harga normal Rp 3.500.000 - Harga diskon Rp 2.500.000 = Penghematan Rp 1.000.000.

5.

Refer to the text in question no. 4. The text implies that the phone is suitable for people who...

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: A

Fitur 'Triple Camera' cocok untuk foto, dan 'Long-lasting battery' cocok untuk bepergian seharian.

6.

Read the text! **URGENTLY REQUIRED** **ENGLISH TUTOR** Requirements: 1. Fluent in English (Active). 2. Minimum D3/S1 in any major. 3. Love children and teaching. 4. Patient and creative. Send your CV to: smart_english@email.com Before March 20th. Which statement is TRUE based on the text?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: C

Syarat nomor 1 adalah 'Fluent in English (Active)', yang berarti pelamar harus bisa berbicara bahasa Inggris.

7.

Refer to the text in question no. 6. 'Patient and creative.' The opposite meaning of 'Patient' is...

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: B

Lawan kata (antonym) dari 'Patient' (sabar) adalah 'Impatient' (tidak sabar).

8.

Read the text! **CLEAN & SHINE LAUNDRY** Too busy to wash your clothes? Let us help you! - Wash & Fold: Rp 6.000/kg - Wash & Iron: Rp 8.000/kg - Dry Cleaning available. - Free pick up and delivery for min. 10 kg. Call us: 0812-3456-7890 What benefit will customers get if they have 12 kg of laundry?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: C

Jika laundry 12 kg (di atas min. 10 kg), pelanggan mendapatkan 'Free pick up and delivery', artinya mereka tidak perlu pergi ke toko laundry (dijemput dan diantar).

9.

Refer to the text in question no. 8. 'Let **us** help you!' The word 'us' refers to...

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: C

Kata 'us' merujuk pada penyedia jasa, yaitu pihak Clean & Shine Laundry.

10.

Which of the following is NOT typically found in an advertisement?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: D

Langkah-langkah membongkar produk biasanya ada di manual/procedure text, bukan di iklan. Iklan fokus pada keunggulan dan bujukan.

11.

Read the text! **MILK-VITA** High Calcium & Low Fat. Best for your bone health. Consume daily for better growth. **Suitable for teenagers.** What is the main benefit of consuming the product?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: B

Tertulis jelas 'Best for your bone health' (Terbaik untuk kesehatan tulangmu).

12.

Read the text! **HOTEL SANTIKA** Located near the beautiful Kuta Beach. Facilities: - Swimming Pool - Free Wi-Fi - Breakfast included - Airport Shuttle Book now at www.hotelsantika.com The text highlights that the hotel is strategically located because...

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: C

Keunggulan lokasi (strategically located) ditunjukkan dengan kalimat 'Located near the beautiful Kuta Beach'.

13.

Refer to the text in question no. 12. 'Airport **Shuttle**'. The word 'Shuttle' means...

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: B

Airport Shuttle adalah layanan transportasi antar-jemput ke bandara.

14.

Read the text! **MUSIC FESTIVAL 2024** Featuring: Top Local Bands. Date: Sunday, 15th May 2024 Venue: City Park Stadium Ticket: Rp 50.000 (Presale), Rp 75.000 (On the spot) If you buy the ticket on the day of the event, how much should you pay?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: C

Membeli di hari acara sama dengan 'On the spot', harganya Rp 75.000.

15.

What kind of language is mostly used in an advertisement?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: B

Iklan menggunakan bahasa persuasif (membujuk) dan umumnya menggunakan Simple Present Tense.

16.

Read the text! **FRESH & COOL AC REPAIR** Does your AC not feel cold? Is it leaking? Call us! We fix all brands. Expert Technicians. **Warranty 30 days.** What does 'Warranty 30 days' mean?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: C

Garansi 30 hari artinya jika ada masalah lagi dalam kurun waktu 30 hari, perbaikan ditanggung penyedia jasa (gratis).

17.

Refer to the text in question no. 16. 'We **fix** all brands.' The synonym of the underlined word is...

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: C

Fix bersinonim dengan Repair (memperbaiki).

18.

Read the text! **HERBAL TOOTHPASTE** Made from natural mint and betel leaf. Helps prevent cavities and bad breath. **Safe for daily use.** The advertisement claims that the product can solve the problem of...

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: C

'Bad breath' artinya bau mulut atau smelly mouth.

19.

Refer to the text in question no. 18. 'Prevent' means...

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: A

Prevent artinya mencegah, atau menghentikan sesuatu agar tidak terjadi.

20.

Which phrase indicates a 'Limited Offer'?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: C

'While stock lasts' (selama persediaan masih ada) menandakan penawaran terbatas (limited).

21.

Read the text! **ROOM FOR RENT** Cozy room for students. Near University of Jakarta. Full furnished (Bed, Cupboard, Desk). AC and Wifi included. Rp 1.500.000 / month. Contact: Mr. Ahmad (0811...) Who is the target audience for this advertisement?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: C

Tertulis 'Cozy room for students' dan 'Near University', jadi targetnya adalah mahasiswa.

22.

Refer to the text in question no. 21. What facility is NOT mentioned in the text?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: D

Bed, AC, Wifi (Internet) disebutkan. Private Kitchen tidak disebutkan.

23.

Read the text! **PIZZA YUMMY** Delivery Order Only. All Pizzas are 20% OFF today! Code: YUMMY20 How can we buy the pizza?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: B

Tertulis 'Delivery Order Only', artinya hanya bisa pesan antar, tidak bisa makan di tempat (Dine-in).

24.

Why do advertisements often use colorful pictures and catchy slogans?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: B

Gambar dan slogan menarik bertujuan menarik perhatian (attract attention).

25.

Read the text! **LOST CAT** Name: Fluffy. White fur, blue eyes. Last seen: Mawar Street. Reward: Rp 500.000. Call: 0812... What should you do if you find the cat?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: C

Jika menemukan kucing hilang sesuai iklan, tindakan yang diharapkan adalah menghubungi nomor yang tertera.

[IKLAN TENGAH ARTIKEL]

B. Essay

Esai 1.

Explain the general structure/parts of an advertisement text!

Lihat Jawaban Lengkap

Pembahasan:

The general structure of an advertisement usually consists of: 1. Title/Name of Product: The name of the product or service. 2. Explanation/Description: Details about the product's features, benefits, or specifications. 3. Persuasive/Selling Point: Discounts, special offers, or unique qualities. 4. Contact Information: Phone number, address, website, or social media where customers can buy or ask.

Esai 2.

Read the text below and identify: a) The Product, b) The Target Audience, c) The Contact Info. 'Glow Up Face Wash. Removes oil and acne instantly! Best for oily skin. Get yours at the nearest pharmacy or call 1500-GLOW.'

Lihat Jawaban Lengkap

Pembahasan:

a) The Product: Glow Up Face Wash. b) The Target Audience: People with oily skin and acne problems. c) The Contact Info: Nearest pharmacy or call 1500-GLOW.

Esai 3.

Write a short advertisement slogan for a 'Spicy Fried Chicken' product!

Lihat Jawaban Lengkap

Pembahasan:

Sample Answer (Creativity based): 'Feel the Burn, Taste the Joy! The Spiciest Chicken in Town!'

Esai 4.

Compare these two offers. Which one is cheaper for a single item purchase? Offer A: Buy 1 Get 1 Free (Price Rp 100.000). Offer B: 30% Discount (Price Rp 60.000).

Lihat Jawaban Lengkap

Pembahasan:

Analysis: - Offer A: You pay Rp 100.000 for 2 items. So, 1 item = Rp 50.000. - Offer B: You pay 70% of Rp 60.000 = Rp 42.000 for 1 item. Conclusion: Offer B is cheaper if you only want to calculate the cost per single item/unit, but Offer A gives more items.

Esai 5.

Analyze the meaning of the phrase 'Terms and Conditions Apply' often found in advertisements.

Lihat Jawaban Lengkap

Pembahasan:

It means that the offer, discount, or promise in the advertisement is not absolute. There are specific rules, requirements, or limitations (like time limit, minimum purchase, specific user) that the customer must fulfill to get the benefit.

Esai 6.

Translate the following advertisement sentence into Indonesian: 'Book your ticket now before it's sold out! Limited seats available.'

Lihat Jawaban Lengkap

Pembahasan:

Pesan tiketmu sekarang sebelum kehabisan! Tempat duduk terbatas.

Esai 7.

Why is it important to include 'testimonials' or 'reviews' in an online advertisement?

Lihat Jawaban Lengkap

Pembahasan:

Testimonials or reviews build trust (credibility). When potential customers see that other people have bought and liked the product, they are more likely to believe the advertisement and make a purchase.

Esai 8.

Create a simple Job Vacancy advertisement for a 'Chef' position at a restaurant. Include at least 2 requirements.

Lihat Jawaban Lengkap

Pembahasan:

**JOB VACANCY: CHEF** We are looking for a professional Chef to join 'Tasty Resto'. Requirements: 1. Minimum 2 years of experience in cooking. 2. Able to work in a team and under pressure. Send your CV to tastyresto@email.com.

Esai 9.

Find the synonym of the underlined words: 'The car is very **economical** and **spacious**.'

Lihat Jawaban Lengkap

Pembahasan:

Economical = Cheap / Cost-effective / Fuel-efficient. Spacious = Large / Roomy / Big.

Esai 10.

If an advertisement says 'satisfaction guaranteed or your money back', what does the seller promise?

Lihat Jawaban Lengkap

Pembahasan:

The seller promises that if the customer is not happy or satisfied with the product quality, the seller will return the money (refund) to the customer.
