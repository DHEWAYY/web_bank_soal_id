A. Pilihan Ganda

1.

Read the text carefully! Once upon a time in West Java, lived a woman named Dayang Sumbi. She had a son named Sangkuriang. One day, Sangkuriang went hunting but returned empty-handed. Fearing his mother's anger, he killed his faithful dog, Tumang, and brought the meat to his mother. What is the primary purpose of the text above?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: B

Tujuan komunikatif (social function) dari teks naratif (Narrative Text) adalah untuk menghibur (to amuse/entertain) pembaca dengan sebuah cerita.

2.

Refer to the text in question no. 1. Why did Sangkuriang kill Tumang?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: C

Berdasarkan teks, Sangkuriang pulang dengan tangan kosong (empty-handed) dan takut ibunya marah (fearing his mother's anger), sehingga ia membunuh Tumang sebagai pengganti hasil buruan.

3.

Malin Kundang became a very rich merchant. He married a beautiful woman. One day, his ship landed on his hometown. His mother recognized him, but he denied her. He said, 'I don't have a dirty and poor mother like you!' What character trait does Malin Kundang show in the text?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: B

Malin Kundang menolak mengakui ibunya karena miskin dan kotor. Sifat ini menunjukkan kedurhakaan (rebellious) dan kesombongan (arrogant).

4.

The generic structure of a narrative text consists of...

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: C

Struktur umum teks naratif adalah Orientation (pengenalan), Complication (masalah), Resolution (penyelesaian), dan opsional Re-orientation.

5.

In the story of 'Timun Mas', who gave the giant seeds to the old widow?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: B

Dalam cerita rakyat Timun Mas, raksasa hijau atau Buto Ijo-lah yang memberikan benih mentimun kepada janda tua tersebut dengan syarat.

6.

Rearrange these jumbled sentences into a good paragraph! 1. She found a giant footprint. 2. Mbok Srini went to the jungle. 3. She prayed to God to give her a child. 4. Buto Ijo passed by and heard her prayer.

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: A

Urutan logis cerita: Mbok Srini ke hutan (2), berdoa minta anak (3), Buto Ijo lewat mendengar (4), (biasanya setelah itu interaksi terjadi). Opsi A paling masuk akal secara kronologis.

7.

'Long time ago, in a small village near the beach in West Sumatra, lived a woman and her son.' This part of the text is called...

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: C

Bagian yang memperkenalkan waktu (Long time ago), tempat (West Sumatra), dan tokoh (woman and her son) disebut Orientation.

8.

What is the moral value of 'The Legend of Toba Lake'?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: A

Pesan moral utama Danau Toba adalah tentang pentingnya menepati janji. Toba melanggar janjinya kepada istrinya untuk tidak menceritakan asal-usulnya (sebagai ikan).

9.

Choose the correct sentence using the Simple Past Tense.

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: B

Narrative text menggunakan Simple Past Tense (V2). Bentuk lampau dari 'meet' adalah 'met'.

10.

The resolution of 'Bawang Merah and Bawang Putih' usually involves...

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: C

Resolusi cerita ini biasanya adalah Bawang Merah dan ibunya mendapat hukuman berupa labu yang berisi binatang berbisa karena keserakahan mereka.

11.

'The boat turned into a big stone.' The synonym of the word 'turned' is...

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: B

Dalam konteks ini, 'turned into' berarti berubah menjadi. Sinonim yang tepat adalah 'changed'.

12.

Why is 'The Legend of Tangkuban Perahu' considered a folktale?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: B

Folktale adalah cerita rakyat yang diturunkan dari generasi ke generasi dan seringkali menjelaskan asal-usul fenomena alam.

13.

Which of the following is NOT a typical character in Indonesian folktales?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: C

Robot adalah elemen cerita fiksi ilmiah (Sci-Fi), bukan cerita rakyat tradisional (folktales) Indonesia.

14.

In the story 'Cindelaras', what animal helped him to reveal his identity?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: A

Cindelaras memiliki ayam jago (rooster) yang berkokok menceritakan asal-usul Cindelaras sebagai putra raja.

15.

Direct Speech: The Giant shouted, 'I will catch you, Timun Mas!' Indirect Speech: ...

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: B

Perubahan ke Indirect Speech dalam masa lampau: 'will' berubah menjadi 'would'. Pronoun 'I' berubah menjadi 'he' (The Giant).

16.

The part of the story where the problem is solved is called...

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: D

Resolution adalah bagian di mana masalah dalam cerita terselesaikan (baik happy ending maupun sad ending).

17.

What did Sangkuriang build to marry Dayang Sumbi?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: B

Syarat Dayang Sumbi adalah membendung sungai Citarum (dam) dan membuat perahu besar (boat) dalam satu malam.

18.

The word 'Once upon a time' is an adverb of...

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: C

'Once upon a time' adalah keterangan waktu (Adverb of Time) yang khas digunakan dalam dongeng.

19.

What distinguishes a Fable from a Legend?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: A

Fabel berfokus pada tokoh hewan yang berperilaku seperti manusia, sedangkan Legenda biasanya menceritakan asal-usul suatu tempat atau tokoh sejarah.

20.

'She was banished from the kingdom.' The word 'banished' means...

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: B

Banished artinya diusir atau dibuang dari suatu tempat. Sinonimnya adalah expelled atau sent away.

21.

In 'The Golden Snail' (Keong Mas), who transformed the princess into a snail?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: C

Dalam cerita Keong Mas, Putri Candra Kirana dikutuk menjadi keong mas oleh seorang penyihir jahat atas suruhan saudaranya.

22.

Which conjunction is commonly used to show the sequence of events in a narrative text?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: B

'Then' (kemudian/lalu) adalah konjungsi temporal yang digunakan untuk menunjukkan urutan kejadian.

23.

The conflict in a story is also known as...

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: C

Konflik atau masalah dalam struktur teks naratif disebut Complication.

24.

Text: 'Roro Jonggrang asked Bandung Bondowoso to build 1000 temples.' What is the passive voice form of this sentence?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: B

Kalimat aktif menggunakan Simple Past (asked). Bentuk pasifnya: Object + was/were + V3 + by Subject. Jadi, 'Bandung Bondowoso was asked...'.

25.

Why did Dayang Sumbi hit Sangkuriang's head when he was a child?

Lihat Pembahasan

Jawaban: B

Dayang Sumbi memukul kepala Sangkuriang karena marah mengetahui bahwa hati yang dimakannya adalah hati Tumang, anjing kesayangan yang sebenarnya adalah ayah Sangkuriang.

[IKLAN TENGAH ARTIKEL]

B. Essay

Esai 1.

Explain the generic structure of a Narrative Text and describe what happens in each part!

Lihat Jawaban Lengkap

Pembahasan:

The generic structure of a Narrative Text consists of: 1. Orientation: Introduces the characters, setting (time and place), and the initial situation. 2. Complication: The part where the problems or conflicts arise among the characters. 3. Resolution: The problem is resolved, either in a happy ending or a sad ending. 4. Re-orientation (Optional): A closing remark or the moral lesson of the story.

Esai 2.

Change the following sentence into Indirect Speech: Malin's mother said, "I curse you into a stone because you are a rebellious son!"

Lihat Jawaban Lengkap

Pembahasan:

Malin's mother said that she cursed him into a stone because he was a rebellious son.

Esai 3.

Mention 3 (three) language features of Narrative Text!

Lihat Jawaban Lengkap

Pembahasan:

1. Using Past Tense (e.g., killed, drank, went). 2. Using Adverbs of Time (e.g., Once upon a time, long ago, one day). 3. Using Action Verbs (e.g., walk, sleep, run). 4. Using Direct Speech.

Esai 4.

Read the short summary: 'Timun Mas ran away from the Giant. She threw salt, and it turned into a sea. The Giant had to swim but he could still chase her.' Analyze the specific function of the 'salt' in the complication of the story!

Lihat Jawaban Lengkap

Pembahasan:

The salt functions as a magical weapon given by the hermit to protect Timun Mas. In the complication, when thrown, it creates an obstacle (turns into a sea) to slow down the Giant, allowing Timun Mas more time to escape.

Esai 5.

Compare the characters of Bawang Merah and Bawang Putih. How are they different?

Lihat Jawaban Lengkap

Pembahasan:

Bawang Putih is described as kind, diligent, patient, and humble. She does all the housework without complaining. On the other hand, Bawang Merah is described as lazy, greedy, arrogant, and jealous of Bawang Putih.

Esai 6.

What can we learn from the story of 'Sangkuriang'? Explain your answer!

Lihat Jawaban Lengkap

Pembahasan:

We can learn that we should not lie to our parents (as Sangkuriang lied about the dog). Also, we must control our emotions and anger (as Dayang Sumbi hit her son), because anger can lead to regret and separation.

Esai 7.

Create a short alternative ending (Resolution) for the story of 'Malin Kundang' where he does not turn into a stone!

Lihat Jawaban Lengkap

Pembahasan:

(Student's creativity) Example: When his mother prayed, a huge storm hit Malin's ship. Malin was terrified and realized his mistake. He ran to his mother, fell to his knees, and cried for forgiveness. His mother, seeing his sincerity, forgave him. The storm stopped, and Malin became a good son who took care of his mother forever.

Esai 8.

Identify the Adverb of Time and Action Verb in this sentence: 'One day, the prince hunted a deer in the dark forest.'

Lihat Jawaban Lengkap

Pembahasan:

Adverb of Time: One day. Action Verb: Hunted.

Esai 9.

Why did Roro Jonggrang cheat by asking the women to pound rice and burn straw?

Lihat Jawaban Lengkap

Pembahasan:

Roro Jonggrang cheated because she did not want to marry Bandung Bondowoso, who had killed her father. She tricked him by making it look like dawn (morning) had arrived so that the spirits helping Bondowoso would leave before finishing the 1000 temples.

Esai 10.

Translate the following sentence into Indonesian: 'The hermit gave a bundle of magical items to the girl so she could survive.'

Lihat Jawaban Lengkap

Pembahasan:

Pertapa itu memberikan sebuah bungkusan berisi benda-benda ajaib kepada gadis itu agar dia bisa bertahan hidup.
